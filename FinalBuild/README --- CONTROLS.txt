By default the movement settings are as follows:
- The right thumbstick can moved upwards to initiate teleportation movement.
- Press the secondary action button on the right hand controller to switch to continuous movement.
- Use the left stick to move with continuous movement.
- Press the secondary action button once more to switch back to teleportation movement.

Grab interations are as follows:
- Grab the sword by initiating a grab action with either hand.
- Hold the weapon in both hands by grabbing onto the sword object with your other hand.
- Release the weapon with your dominant hand to switch the sword grip to the other hand.
